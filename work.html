Stories and projects

Long-form, exclusive and investigative storytelling, data-driven visuals, immersive explainers. Elevated through craft, planning and deep collaboration. Work I’ve led or contributed to has regularly earned industry recognition for design excellence and innovation.


>A rare look at the devastation caused by AR-15 shootings

Designed a 38-page tabloid-size print special section for Sunday subscribers; personally handled adapting print from digital given the potentially traumatizing material. Included with the entry that won the Pulitzer Prize for National Reporting, recognized as Best in Show by the Society for News Design and awarded a Gold Medal from the Society of Publication Designers. (The Washington Post, December 23, 2023)

Expertise

What I do: I specialize in transforming complex reporting into clear, compelling visual experiences. Whether leading teams on enterprise investigations or stepping in to design hands-on, I focus on craft, collaboration, and strategic thinking that aligns creative ambition with real-world constraints.

My approach: Great visual journalism requires more than good design—it needs early collaboration, smart scoping, and a deep understanding of what makes stories connect with audiences. I work across departments to shape concepts, guide creative direction, and ensure projects are both beautiful and achievable.

Recognition: My work has contributed to two Pulitzer Prizes and earned numerous industry honors, including Best in Show recognition from the Society for News Design. But the real measure is the impact: stories that inform, move, and stick with readers

What I'm looking for: Media or communications jobs, and open to consulting, freelancing or teaching.
